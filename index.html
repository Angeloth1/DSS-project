DSS Project P2019095

Our goal is to enhance your experience with the Ionian University StudentBank Japan branch. You will be asked a series of questions that will enable our Decision Support System (DSS) to assess your financial situation and determine your eligibility for credit approval. The system has been meticulously developed using a comprehensive dataset from UCL , ensuring robustness and accuracy.


DISCLAIMER:

  • We strictly adhere to a policy of not retaining any of your personal information.
  • The output of this tool should be understood as a suggestion intended solely to support our decision-making process. It serves a singular purpose of providing assistance to our system.
